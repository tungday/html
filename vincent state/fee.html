PRIMARY

The Tuition fees for june and the first term fees must be paid before the end of May. This applies even to those who are seeking financial assistance form the school. Those in receipt of Government Concession are promoted to the next higher class need not pay fees.

Fees will be paid through the South Indian Bank. Fee bills will be available online which parents can print form the website and pay to the bank by cash

Where possible, parents are requested to pay fees ... struck off the rolls.
